Sakura Haruno (Japanese: 春野 サクラ, Hepburn: Haruno Sakura) is a fictional
character in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto.
Sakura is depicted as a kunoichi affiliated with Konohagakure (木ノ葉隠れの里,
English version: "Hidden Leaf Village") and a part of Team 7, which consists of herself,
Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and their sensei Kakashi Hatake. Besides the main
series, Sakura has appeared in several pieces of the Naruto media, most notably
the spin-off Naruto: The Seventh Hokage and the Scarlet Spring (2015) and the sequel
Boruto: Naruto Next Generations (2016) where she married Sasuke Uchiha taking
name of Sakura Uchiha (Japanese: うちは サクラ, Hepburn: Uchiha Sakura)
and is the mother of their daughter, Sarada Uchiha.[1]
